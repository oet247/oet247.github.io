Hi. 

I'm oet247[^1] and this is my blog, the name of which I've taken liberal, bordering on some sort of creative theft, inspiration from Nicolas Creamer's [Wrong Every Time](https://wrongeverytime.com/).

I've always wanted to have my own website to put my writing on instead of toiling away in the pits of hell (Google Docs), and a friend of mine (hi lou) inspired me to try with her own website here [lou's blog](https://glhf.lildro.blog/).

It might be already too late to mention this, but excuse any grammatical or spelling mistakes on this page and in any of the posts - English is neither my native language, nor do I have someone with enough time on their hands to comb through even a fraction of my deranged writing. If you get annoyed easily by errors of the tongue, proceed with caution past this point, I can not promise your safety, but hopefully you can move past my ineptitude and take the plunge.

I will try my best to give a one, admitedly very windy, sentence outline of this blog's intended contents[^3]. The posts will bounce around a variety of different topcis, the main ones being: 
essays on YOMI Hustle, and getting good at all manners of games in general, but that one is of particularly interest; analysis pieces on media, which if I were to order by estimated descending frequency of output, would be: anime, movies, games, books, manga; and whatever else I feel like writing really. Some of my other interests include - beatboxing, learning japanese, drawing, table tennis and mathematics. 

Partially to rep my nerd credentials, and with the additional functionality of serving as a highly personal kind of sanity check, I will also give you non-numbered and thus internet unfriendly, yet still quasi ordered within sections, list of my roughly favourite things in each of the above categories, including some others:
- anime[^4] - Shoujo Kakumei Utena, Oregairu, Violet Evergarden, Ping Pong, Mob Psycho 100, Shin Seiki Evangelion
- movies - Koe no Katachi, Sennen Joyuu
- games - The Beginner's Guide, Getting Over It with Bennet Foddy, Milk outside a bag of milk outside a bag of milk, Signalis
- books - Konbini Ningen, Mata Onaji Yume wo Miteita, Good Habits Bad Habits, NHK ni Youkoso!, Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy
- manga - Medalist, Yotsuba to!, Kimi to Tsuzuru Utakata, Kokou no Hito
- blogs/newsletters - [pluralistic](https://pluralistic.net/), [Where's your Ed at?](https://www.wheresyoured.at/), [Wrong Every Time](https://wrongeverytime.com/)
- music - Kessoku Bando (yeah yeah I got it already shut up), Kinoko Teikoku, The Pillows, Yorushika, Soutaisei Riron, La Larks
- beatboxers[^5] - I was gonna write a whole list, but it ended up just being french dudes

- youtubers - Folding Ideas, 3Blue1Brown, Fractal Philosophy, Hbomberguy, Melee Stats
